VIDEOGRAPHY PORTFOLIO

This was the first short film I've ever made. I tried to capture the struggles of a bad relationship. It was a bit dark, but I still like how it turned out. I tried to use different camera angles, audio, color grading, and facial expressions to help me tell the story. I tried to use as little dialogue as possible. The only words uttered in the video was "bring my food to the living room, I'm hungry." Some feedback I've gotten was to not show the face of the guy to "vilify him" even more. That was definitely valid feedback that I plan to implement in the future.
I was traveling to see family in the west coast and had a ton of footage and I wanted to see if I could condense it to a short travel video. This was the first travel video I've ever produced. Canon 80D was used as the main camera, GoPro Hero 6 was used for the under water shots, and the DJI Spark was the drone I choose for the aerial shots.
